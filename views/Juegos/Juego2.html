About PUG-B

PUG-B is a quirky and exciting battle royale game that puts players in the paws of adorable pugs competing to be the last one standing. Set in a pug-filled world with hilarious weapons and power-ups, this game is a lighthearted take on the battle royale genre, perfect for dog lovers and competitive gamers alike.
