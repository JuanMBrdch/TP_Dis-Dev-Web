About Charter: Lost in the City

Charter: Lost in the City is a third-person adventure game that follows a tourist stranded in a foreign city. The protagonist must navigate through bustling streets, solve puzzles, and interact with locals to find his way back to the airport before his flight departs. With stunning visuals, a gripping narrative, and a diverse, living city to explore, the game offers a rich, immersive experience for those who love adventure and exploration.
