About Doot: Eternal

Doot: Eternal is a high-octane, retro-style first-person shooter that pays homage to classic boomer shooters like Doom. In this chaotic and humorous game, you battle against waves of enemies wielding obnoxious musical instruments. From blaring trumpets to screeching violins, each enemy creates an audio assault while you blast through levels filled with challenging combat and non-stop action.
