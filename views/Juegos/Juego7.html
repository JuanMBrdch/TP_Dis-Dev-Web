About Oxodized

Oxodized is a unique blend of FPS, base-building, and survival gameplay set in a harsh, open-world environment. Players must scavenge for resources, build fortified bases, and fend off rival players in a free-for-all struggle to survive. With its dynamic weather, resource scarcity, and strategic combat, Oxodized challenges players to master both survival skills and tactical shooting.
