About Mega Impact Siblings: Squabble

Mega Impact Siblings: Squabble is a fast-paced, competitive fighting game where players assume the roles of sibling characters in a high-energy battle for supremacy. Developed by BrightWorks Studios, the game has taken the eSports world by storm, boasting a unique combination of strategic gameplay and humorous sibling rivalry mechanics that keep players engaged.
