About Conter Estraike 3

Conter Estraike 3 is a tactical first-person shooter that brings players into intense, strategic combat environments. Inspired by the popular CS:GO, this game takes team-based shooting to a whole new level with enhanced graphics, more realistic physics, and expanded tactical gameplay.
