About Stealy Wheely Automobiley

Stealy Wheely Automobiley is an immersive open-world crime simulator that takes players into the gritty streets of the UK. With a focus on car theft, heists, and exploration, players can freely roam a detailed urban landscape, engaging in high-speed chases, cunning escapes, and strategic missions. The game combines realism with dark humor, offering an unforgettable sandbox experience for fans of the genre.
